#### Big Purple Dot

#### Total Expert
- Purpose-built lead management (auto-capture, routing, assignment); integration with Customer Intelligence, servicing portfolios, referral sources

#### LendingPad
- **Primarily LOS** but includes CRM: compliance check, document archiving, custom workflows, lead/campaign management
- Task segmentation (processor, manager, closer, funder, post-closer roles); real-time updates; cloud-based fast implementation

#### Floify
- **POS (Point-of-Sale) specialist**: white-labeled 1003 app, document upload, eSign disclosures, loan status portal for borrowers
- Business Rules automation (if-this-then-that); mobile PWA with customizable branding; integrations with 50+ platforms (Jungo, Surefire, Aidium)

## PART 2: SYNTHESIZED MUST-HAVE FEATURES FOR LOOPENTA

### 1. UNIVERSAL (All Orgs: Mortgage, Realtor, Referral Partner, Title, Escrow)

**Contact & Lead Management**
- Centralized contact database with custom fields (by role/org type)
- Bulk import (CSV, API, integrations)
- Contact tagging/segmentation
- Phone + email + custom communication channel fields
- Deal/transaction association (linked to multiple deals or single deal)

**Communication Hub**
- Email (templates, bulk send, drip campaigns)
- SMS/text (compliant with TCPA, DNC lists)
- In-app messaging + task assignment
- Communication history logged to contact record
- Pre-built templates by use case (follow-up, education, nurture)

**Pipeline & Status Tracking**
- Customizable deal/transaction pipeline stages
- Drag-drop status updates
- Timeline view (closing date, milestones)
- Activity feed per deal (who did what, when)

**Task & Workflow Management**
- Task assignment (to individual or role)
- Reminders + due dates

**Compliance & Audit Trail**
- Activity logging (who accessed what, when)

- GDPR-compliant data retention/deletion policies

**Mobile Access**
- Web-responsive design (works on mobile browser)
- Core features accessible on mobile (contact lookup, task assignment, communication, status update)

**Reporting & Dashboards**
- Activity dashboard (calls made, emails sent, tasks completed)
- Pipeline summary (deals by stage, closing timeline)
- Team activity (per person, per team, per org)
- Custom reports (export to CSV/PDF)

### 2. MORTGAGE-SPECIFIC (Home Loan Advisor / Loan Officer, Manager, Processor)

**Lead Capture & Qualification**
- Web lead capture forms (for LOs' websites)
- Lead source tracking (referral, Zillow, website, direct, internal referral)
- Lead scoring (simple: hot/warm/cold OR AI: likelihood to fund within X days)
- Auto-assignment by loan officer or team

**Rate & Product Management**
- Daily rate sheet upload (display best rates for common scenarios)
- Pricing matrices (by credit score, LTV, loan type)
- Pre-approval letter generation (auto-populate with borrower info, rates, terms)
- Rate lock tracking

**Loan Pipeline & Milestones**
- Loan stages: Pre-App → Application → Processing → Underwriting → Clear-to-Close → Funded → Servicing
- Auto-trigger milestone reminders (e.g., "sent disclosures" → remind processor in 3 days)
- Expected close date tracking
- Loan-to-value (LTV) + credit score visibility

**Document Management**
- Secure document vault (1003, pay stubs, tax returns, appraisals, conditional approval, clear-to-close docs)
- Document requirement checklist (auto-populate by loan type)
- Document status tracking ("pending from borrower," "received," "reviewed")
- eSign integration for disclosures (TRID compliance)

**Referral Partner Tracking** (within mortgage org)
- Track referrals from CPAs, financial advisors, insurance agents, other LOs
- Referral fee tracking + payout schedule
- Referral performance dashboard (referrer A closed 5 loans, avg $400K)

**Compliance Automation**
- TRID compliance: auto-generate Loan Estimate, Closing Disclosure with correct timing
- RESPA safeguards: auto-flag compensation arrangements
- Interest rate lock protection (rate lock expiry reminders)
- Appraisal management integration (track appraisal order → receipt → issues)

**Reporting for Loan Officers**
- Personal pipeline (my open loans, closing dates, status)
- Loan officer dashboard (# funded this month, avg close time, referral sources)
- Processor handoff reports (queue of loans awaiting processing)

**Reporting for Managers**
- Team pipeline (all LOs, all loans, by stage)
- Production metrics (funded loans, volume, average close time, referral source breakdown)
- LO performance comparison (leaderboard style: funded loans, volume, efficiency)
- Pipeline health (% in underwriting overdue, % past close date)

### 6. CROSS-ORG NETWORKING FEATURES (Lead Routing, Co-Marketing, Shared Pipeline Visibility)

**Lead Routing & Referral**
- Realtor identifies buyer → create referral to LO (with borrower pre-qual data)
- LO identifies opportunity for referral partner (e.g., CPA client needing mortgage) → send lead
- Referral interface: pick org type/name, add note, one-click send
- Auto-populate referral with deal context (property price, loan amount estimate, urgency)

**Cross-Org Pipeline Visibility** (Opt-In)
- Realtor can see their referred loan's status: "Application submitted," "In underwriting," "Clear-to-close," "Funded"
- LO can see realtor contact info for the transaction (commission broker, agent phone)
- LO can message realtor from within deal (task assignment: "Need title commitment," mark when received)

**Co-Branded Marketing Materials**
- Realtor logo + LO logo on marketing flyer (e.g., "Get pre-approved with [LO] while working with [Realtor]")
- Email templates (realtor can send "mortgage partner" intro to buyer database)
- Landing page (if buyer comes through realtor's website, landing page mentions pre-approval option)

**Shared Deal Timeline**
- Single shared deal record (one view of truth)
- All parties see: property address, sale price, estimated close date, status
- Comments/updates visible to all (realtor posts: "Offer accepted," LO posts: "Clear to close," etc.)

**Compliance Safeguards for Cross-Org**
- Referral partner cannot see deal details without explicit LO/realtor permission
- Title company can only see deals they're assigned to close
- Escrow company cannot see full borrower SSN or credit details
- Audit trail: who accessed what, when

### 8. COMPLIANCE CONSIDERATIONS

**TRID/RESPA (Mortgage)**
- Loan Estimate auto-generated within 1 business day of application + TRID-compliant formatting
- Closing Disclosure auto-generated 3+ business days before close + correct calculations
- No QC loan product changes without redisclosure
- Compensation tracking (prevent RESPA yield spread premium violations)
- Appraisal independence (independent appraiser selected, no LO pressure)

**Fair Housing (Realtor)**
- No discriminatory language in marketing/outreach (auto-check templates)
- Showing statistics (track which agents show properties to whom; detect steering patterns)
- Lead source diversity reporting (are some agents receiving leads only from certain sources?)

**TCPA/CCPA (All)**
- Do-Not-Call list check before SMS/call campaigns
- SMS opt-in consent required (capture in CRM)
- Email unsubscribe link on all bulk emails (auto-included in templates)
- Annual compliance audit log export
- Data deletion on request (California residents, EU citizens if applicable)

**State-Specific Mortgage Rules**
- Unique loan program restrictions (e.g., TX limits on cash-out refinances)
- Licensing requirements (state-level LO licensing validation)
- Rate/fee lock periods (display prominently; auto-remind when about to expire)

**E-Signature & Document Security**
- DocuSign or equivalent integration for eSignature (TRID disclosures, rate lock agreements)
- Encryption for stored documents (at rest + in transit)
- Secure document sharing (one-time URL expiry)
- Audit trail for document access + signing

## PART 3: DESIGN PATTERNS & QUICK WINS

### Page Structure Patterns Observed

**Dashboard Layouts**
- Top: KPI cards (funded count, pipeline total, overdue deals count, team size)
- Left nav: Quick filters (my deals, team deals, all deals, overdue)
- Main: Deal/contact cards or list rows with status badge, action buttons
- Right sidebar: Details pane (click a card to see full record)

**Deal/Transaction Records**
- Header: Deal identifier (loan#/MLS#), property address, closing date, status badge
- Tabs: Overview | Documents | Communication | Tasks | Compliance
- Overview: key fields in 2-3 columns (party info, amount, dates, stage)
- Each tab loads related sub-records (e.g., Documents tab = file list with upload button)

**Lead Management**
- List view: Lead name, source, status, last activity date, assigned to, action buttons
- Quick actions: Call, Email, Text, Schedule Task, Change Status
- Bulk actions: Mark as "qualified," reassign, add tags, send campaign

**Reporting**
- Report header: Report name, date range, filters applied, export buttons
- Metric cards: Show key numbers + trend indicator (up/down arrow with %)
- Charts: Line graph (trend over time), bar chart (comparison), pie (composition)
- Drill-down: Click a bar/slice to filter to underlying records

### Quick-Win Features (High Impact, Lower Lift)

1. **Pre-built Dashboard Templates**
   - Copy kvCORE's Smart Plans concept: auto-recommend next action based on deal stage/lead behavior
   - "Click to start phone call" + "Leave voicemail" buttons

   - 20–30 pre-built templates (categorized: nurture, follow-up, milestone, referral, post-close)
   - One-click customization (merge fields for name, property address, rate, etc.)

3. **Task Auto-Generation**
   - Define "Post-Application" task template = { send Welcome email, schedule appraisal order, request docs list }
   - On deal stage change, auto-create tasks; manager can bulk-complete or reassign

4. **Mobile Web Responsive**
   - Use CSS media queries; don't build separate native app
   - Mobile nav: hamburger menu, bottom action bar with Call/Email/Text/Task buttons

5. **Integration Stubs (Not Live, But API-Ready)**
   - Zillow lead capture form (capture form submission → CRM contact)
   - Zapier integration (allow customers to connect their own tools)
   - Google Calendar sync read-only (show next 7 days on dashboard)

6. **Referral Partner Directory**
   - Admin creates/manages referral partner orgs (LOs, realtors, CPAs, title companies in network)
   - Realtor can search directory by name; click to view contact info + rating (# successful referrals)

7. **Simple Compliance Checklist**
   - Per deal type, show required documents/steps (e.g., FHA loan = appraisal required, no gift funds exception letter, etc.)
   - Checkboxes for manager to verify; generates compliance certificate for audit

## PART 4: ARCHITECTURE IMPLICATIONS

### Data Model Sketch
```
Organization
  - org_type: "mortgage" | "realtor" | "referral_partner" | "title" | "escrow"
  - name, address, phone, website
  - roles: [Manager, Loan Officer, Processor] | [Broker, Agent, TC] | [Admin]

Deal/Transaction
  - deal_id, org_id (primary org managing deal)
  - deal_type: "purchase" | "refi" | "cash_out"
  - property_address, sale_price, loan_amount
  - borrower_contact_id, realtor_contact_id, processor_id, underwriter_id
  - stage: "pre_app" | "app" | "processing" | ... | "funded"
  - status_updates: [{ timestamp, user_id, new_stage, note }]
  - cross_org_visibility: [{ org_id, access_level: "read" | "read_write" }]

Contact
  - contact_id, org_id
  - name, email, phone
  - contact_type: "borrower" | "realtor" | "loan_officer" | "partner" | "title_company"
  - custom_fields: { key: value }
  - deals: [ deal_id, deal_id, ... ]

Task/Activity
  - task_id, deal_id, assigned_to_user_id
  - title, description, due_date, completed_date
  - linked_documents: [ doc_id, ... ]
```

### Compliance Guardrails (Firestore Rules)
- Org A cannot read Org B's deals unless explicitly cross-org shared
- Referral partner can only see deals they referred (marked with referral_source = partner_org_id)
- Title company can only read deals assigned to them (title_company_id field)
- All reads/writes logged with user_id, timestamp, action, record_id
